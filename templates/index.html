LIC Insurance Plans

Choose from our comprehensive range of LIC insurance plans designed to meet your specific financial goals and protection needs.

Term Plan

Pure protection at affordable premiums. Ideal for young families and income earners.

  • High coverage at low cost
  • Tax benefits under 80C
  • Flexible premium payment

Endowment Plan

Combination of insurance and investment. Guaranteed returns with life coverage.

  • Guaranteed maturity benefits
  • Life insurance coverage
  • Bonus participation

Pension Plan

Secure your retirement with regular pension income after retirement.

  • Regular pension income
  • Vesting benefit
  • Tax advantages

Child Plan

Secure your child's future education and marriage expenses.

  • Education fund creation
  • Waiver of premium
  • Maturity benefits

ULIP

  • Market-linked returns
  • Fund switching options
  • Tax benefits

Health Plan

  • Cashless treatment

  • Family coverage
